The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower located in Paris, France. It was designed by Gustave Eiffel and completed in 1889. The tower is 330 meters tall and is one of the most iconic landmarks in the world.

The Great Wall of China is a series of fortifications made of stone, brick, and other materials. It was built to protect Chinese states and empires from invasions. The wall stretches over 21,000 kilometers and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
